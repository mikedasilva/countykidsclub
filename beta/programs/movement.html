Movement & Fitness Programs

Jump, run, climb, and play your way to confidence. Our programs give kids ages 3–7 a fun, structured space to build coordination, strength, fitness foundations, and teamwork — every single Saturday.

What kids experience

Every class blends movement, fitness, imagination, and teamwork — helping kids grow stronger, more confident, and more connected.

🏃‍♀️

High-energy movement games

Relay races, tag variations, and partner challenges that keep kids moving, laughing, and fully involved — learning without even realizing it.

🧱

Strength, balance & coordination

Crawling, climbing, balancing, and age-appropriate strength activities that build strong bodies, motor skills, and confident movers.

💬

Teamwork & confidence

From group circuits to class huddles, kids learn to listen, cheer for teammates, and feel proud of what their bodies can do.

Movement Program FAQ

Common questions about our movement programs.

My child is between age groups or has a birthday during the program. Which program should they join?
We recommend joining the program that best matches their current abilities and where you think they'll be most comfortable and engaged.
What equipment does my child need?
No special equipment needed. Just something comfortable to move around in and a clean pair of indoor running shoes.
Does my child need to be at a certain fitness level?
No minimum fitness level required. Activities are designed to be challenging but structured so every kid feels engaged and empowered to participate in a fun, supportive environment.
Do I drop my child off or stay with them?
A parent or guardian must stay for the duration of each class. You're welcome to jump in and help, or simply sit back and watch your child play!
What's the format of a typical session?
Each session starts with a quick team huddle to set goals and get everyone ready. After a warm-up, kids move through 3–5 fun activities, each about 8–12 minutes. We take water breaks and gather for mini huddles between activities to keep everyone refreshed and connected.
Are any snacks provided?
We provide a healthy snack after each class. Please make sure your child brings a water bottle to stay hydrated throughout.
What is the adult-to-child ratio?
The program is supported by 3–5 adult volunteers each week, ensuring a safe and well-supervised environment for all participants.
How do I pay for the program?
We use YouthModo for registrations. Visit our registration portal to sign up — payment details are provided during that process.
What if I have a question not covered here?
Email us at countykidsclub@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
