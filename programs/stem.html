STEM & Robotics

Hands-on science, coding, and robotics challenges that spark curiosity and teach kids to think like engineers and problem-solvers — no prior experience required.

What kids will experience

A hands-on, inquiry-based environment where curiosity is celebrated and every experiment is a chance to discover something new.

🏗️

Building & engineering

Design and build structures, simple machines, and robots using age-appropriate kits and materials. Learn what works, what doesn't, and why.

💻

Coding & problem-solving

Introduction to computational thinking through block-based coding, logic puzzles, and guided challenges that make programming accessible and fun.

🔬

Science experiments

Hands-on experiments that explore chemistry, physics, and biology in ways that are surprising, tactile, and genuinely exciting for kids.

STEM Program FAQ

Early questions about our upcoming STEM & Robotics programs.

Does my child need any prior experience with coding or robotics?
Not at all. Programs will be designed for beginners and structured so that kids with no prior experience can participate fully and confidently.
What age groups will STEM programs be for?
We're still finalizing the age breakdown, but expect programs aimed at different stages — from early elementary through middle school. Join the mailing list for updates.
Will kids need to bring their own devices or materials?
No — all materials, devices, and kits will be provided as part of the program. We'll share a full what-to-bring list when registration opens.
How can I get involved or propose a program?
If you're an educator, engineer, or STEM enthusiast interested in running a program, we'd love to hear from you. Email us at countykidsclub@gmail.com.
