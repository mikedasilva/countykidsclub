Special-interest Programs

For kids who want to go deep on something specific. Our special-interest programs cover a range of passions — from chess and art to music and more — led by community experts who love what they teach.

What kids will experience

Special-interest programs are built around a single subject, led by someone who's genuinely expert and passionate about it. Every session goes deep.

🧠

Deep-focus learning

Unlike broad programs, special-interest offerings let kids immerse themselves in one subject — building real competence and confidence in something they're genuinely curious about.

⭐

Expert instruction

Each program is run by a local community member with deep expertise in their field — whether that's a chess player, artist, musician, or specialist of another kind.

🤝

Community & shared interest

Kids who share a passion get to meet and learn alongside each other — building friendships rooted in something they all love.

Special-interest Program FAQ

Common questions about our special-interest programs.

What makes a program "special-interest"?
Special-interest programs are focused on a single topic or skill area, run by community members with specific expertise — a chess player running chess club, an artist running an art program, and so on. They're for kids who want to go deep on something, not just broadly active.
Can I suggest a program topic?
Absolutely — we love hearing ideas from families. If there's something you wish existed for kids in PEC, tell us about it at countykidsclub@gmail.com.
I have expertise in something — how do I propose running a program?
Reach out at countykidsclub@gmail.com and tell us what you'd like to run. We handle the logistics, registration, and venue — you bring the knowledge and passion.
Do special-interest programs cost more than other programs?
Pricing will depend on the program's materials and delivery costs, but we'll always aim to keep things as accessible as possible for PEC families. Specific costs will be listed with each program when registration opens.
