About County Kids Club

We started with a simple question: what if Prince Edward County had a community-run space where young kids could move, play, and grow together?

Our first program launched in 2025 — a Saturday morning movement class for preschoolers and early graders. The response blew us away. Kids came alive, parents stayed and cheered, and what started as a gym class quickly became a community gathering place.

Now we're expanding. County Kids Club is growing into a multi-program club with opportunities across movement and fitness, STEM, nature exploration, and more — all run by local people who care deeply about the kids and families in our community.

As a registered Not-for-Profit Corporation, we keep costs as low as possible, rely on local volunteers and subject-matter experts to run each program, and design everything around one core belief: every kid deserves access to great programs right here in PEC.

Meet the Founding Team

County Kids Club was started by four local parents, coaches, and community members who saw a gap and decided to fill it. We organize and run the club, and recruit passionate local experts to lead each individual program.

Jessica Santos
Sports and movement have always brought me joy, connection, and confidence. As a mom to an active 3-year-old, I noticed there were few places for young kids to explore movement in a fun, supportive way. Our community deserves more opportunities like this, and I'm excited to help create them.
Lara Hilmi
Lara has a lifelong connection to sport that fuels her passion for helping children discover the joy and benefits of movement. She believes deeply in the power of sport as a way to build confidence, leadership, and lasting friendships. When not chasing after her 3 boys, she's running and cycling along the Millennium Trail.
Michael Da Silva
A lifelong fitness enthusiast who loves seeing kids move, grow, and push their limits. A former die-hard skateboarder turned cyclist, runner, soccer, and volleyball player. Passionate about longevity, healthy living, and building strong communities. Find him at the local Repair Cafés and Kids of Steel events.
Robert Spagnola
Sport and physical activity have always been central to Rob's life. As a former athlete, youth sports coach, personal trainer, and owner of The Gym in the Armoury, he brings expertise and genuine enthusiasm to every group he leads.

Frequently Asked Questions

General questions about County Kids Club.

What is County Kids Club?
County Kids Club is a community-run club in Prince Edward County that offers affordable, fun, and enriching programs for kids. We're a registered Not-for-Profit Corporation, 100% volunteer-led, and built around the idea that every kid deserves access to great programs right here in PEC.
Who runs the programs?
The club is organized by our four founding members (Jess, Lara, Mike, and Rob). Each individual program may also have its own dedicated instructors — local experts who are passionate about the subject they teach. You'll find instructor info on each program's page.
What age groups do your programs serve?
It varies by program. Our movement programs currently serve children ages 3–7. As we expand into other program areas, age ranges will differ. Each program page will have specific age details.
How much do programs cost?
We keep costs as low as possible. Our movement programs run $45 per child for a 4-week session. Pricing for other programs will be determined based on what it takes to run them sustainably.
How do I register for a program?
We use YouthModo for all registrations. Visit the program page for a direct link when registration is open, or visit our registration portal.
How do I get notified when new programs launch?
Join our mailing list — it's the best way to hear about new programs before spots fill up. Sign up here.
Can I volunteer or help run a program?
Absolutely — we'd love your help! Whether you want to volunteer at an existing program or propose a new one you could lead, reach out at countykidsclub@gmail.com or sign up to volunteer.
What if I have a question not covered here?
Email us at countykidsclub@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
